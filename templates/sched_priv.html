Team Organizer Discord Bot - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 10, 2025

Team Organizer (#9044) ("the Bot") respects your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled:

1. Data Collection

  • The Bot stores only necessary data for its features, such as team names, event details, and Discord user IDs.
  • No sensitive personal information is collected.

2. Data Usage

  • Data is used solely for providing scheduling, team management, and event features within Discord.
  • Data is not shared, sold, or used for advertising.

3. Data Storage

  • Data is stored securely on the Bot’s server and is only accessible to the Bot owner.
  • Data may be deleted upon request or if you leave the server.

4. Third Parties

  • The Bot does not share your data with third parties.
  • The Bot operates within Discord and is subject to Discord’s Privacy Policy.

5. User Rights

  • You may request deletion of your data by contacting the Bot owner.
  • You may stop using the Bot at any time.

6. Changes

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
For questions or concerns, contact the Bot owner via Discord or the GitHub repository.
